Membership:

Individual: $50/month
Family: $75/month
Student/Retiree: $25/month

Membership gets you 24/7 access.

Becoming a Member:

We usually have an existing member provide a tour which gives you a chance to see what it's about, meet at least one other maker(members also inhabit an internal online forum/chat "Slack" to stay in communication with everyone), fill out our waiver, and answer any questions you may have.

To get started, propose some date/time's you have available by mailing info@appletonmakerspace.org and we'll reach out to the members and coordinate a tour for you.

We are a 100% volunteer community. There are no employees. Everything here happens because someone donates time and energy to make it happen.
